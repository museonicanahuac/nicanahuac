About us

MISION: Our mission is to offer a quality service.

VISION: To be the best reference site in Nicaraguan history, art and culture. Our museum will offer a high degree of quality service and professionalism, offering documentation, historical pieces, art, among others.

STANDARS: The strong point of our museum is and will always be a high degree of responsibility to establish moral, ethical and honest values to all our future visitors.
