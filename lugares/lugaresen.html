TOURIST PLACES OF NICARAGUA






Nicaragua has three regions differentiated by climate, physical environment and population: Pacific Region, Central Region, Caribbean Region, which includes the North Caribbean Coast Autonomous Region (RACCN) and the South Caribbean Coast Autonomous Region (RACCS).

Its immense tourist attractions allow you to develop a variety of activities such as surfing on the Pacific beaches, hiking through volcanoes and magical lagoons where you can see hundreds of species of animals and plants excellent for the practice of ecological tourism. Kayaking in the calm waters of a huge lake and walks through hundreds of islets, fabulous rivers, visiting colonial cities, archaeological treasures, diving in the crystal clear waters of the Caribbean. Travelers can walk paths through the jungle vegetation to meet farms that have facilities for the attention of visitors, can practice hiking, observation and identification of flora and fauna, mountain biking and horseback riding with a deliciously cool climate.

The beauty, natural and cultural variety of our country in the center of the continent, are combined with the hospitality of its people and with a history that moves and inspires. This is a land with a great cultural, multiethnic and multilingual richness where solidarity and optimism flourish and make Nicaragua a Unique, Original land.
