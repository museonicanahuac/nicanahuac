Museums and education maintain a close and indispensable relationship each other, so, museums are privileged places of learning, teaching, and sharing great moments of knowledge, this new knowledge comes from narration of legends, conferences, exhibitions, and a great variety of source of first-hand information for their visitors.

Museums represent a big cultural and social value in every nation, where the arts, science, and research stand out.

When we visit museums, without any exception, our knowledge grows, and we learn more information than we had. On the other hand, museums are important to increase the cultural offer of the places where they are located, constituting an interesting attraction.
