HISTORY OF NICARAGUA






The history of Nicaragua covers the time period from the arrival of Christopher Columbus including the arrival of the first Spanish explorers for colonization and conquest to the present.

Nicaragua is not a large country, but it has a very intense history that is several centuries old. It is a nation with ancient cultures, customs and traditions dating back hundreds of years. A republic that has gone through various stages and historical periods that deserve to be known.
